Our Story

Harvard Learning is a prominent international course development and accreditation body that has been actively shaping the global education landscape since its establishment in 1989. With a rich history spanning over three decades, Harvard Learning has been at the forefront of crafting innovative and internationally recognized educational solutions. Through partnerships with numerous international education companies, Harvard Learning has played a pivotal role in the development and accreditation of courses that cater to a diverse range of learners across the world.

At Harvard Learning, we are committed to fostering educational excellence. Our mission revolves around the seamless integration of quality educational programs and services into the curricula of international educational organizations. We have cultivated a network of collaborators who share our vision of advancing global education standards, making our courses and accreditation services an essential pillar of international education development.
